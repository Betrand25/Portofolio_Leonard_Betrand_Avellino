Bisya

What’s this?

Bisya” is a Figma design prototype for a translator app designed specifically to help deaf individuals. This application aims to bridge the communication gap by translating sign language into text or speech, making it easier for deaf people to interact with other people in various situations. The design focuses on accessibility and easy-to-use features to ensure smooth communication.

What was your role in the group?

This project was conducted as a group assignment for the AI course, and my role was to design the AI prototype for the application that we developed.

What are the benefits of the project

"Bisya" is a Figma prototype for a translator app designed to help deaf people by converting sign language to text or speech, making communication easier in a variety of situations. The design prioritizes accessibility and ease of use for smooth interaction.
