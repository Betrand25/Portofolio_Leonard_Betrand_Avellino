This website prototype design explores the rich diversity of ethnicity, culture, and fauna in Indonesia. It showcases various aspects of Indonesian heritage, including traditional customs, local wildlife, and cultural expressions, offering users an immersive experience in understanding the country's unique identity and biodiversity.

What was your role in the group?

This project was carried out as a group and the task given was to create a web design prototype using Figam. My role in this project was to create the home page, sign in page and login page.

What are the benefits of the project

This website benefits society by promoting cultural awareness and appreciation of Indonesia's diversity, helping to preserve traditions and wildlife. It also serves as an educational tool, encouraging tourism and raising awareness about the country's heritage.
