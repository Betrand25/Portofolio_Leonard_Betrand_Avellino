Money Wise is a program designed to help users track and manage their finances by providing an easy way to view their income and expenses. Users can monitor their financial activity, identify spending patterns, and gain better control over their budgeting, enabling them to make informed financial decisions and plan for the future more effectively

What was your role in the group?

This project was initiated by a UX course assignment. This assignment was done in a group. My role was as a designer on Figma and to develop the mobile website to make it high fidelity.

What are the benefits of the project

The impact of the project I created is that users can manage their finances, both income and expenses, so that users are expected to be able to manage their money more wisely.
