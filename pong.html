Pong Game

What’s this?

The game of Pong is similar to traditional Pong, but stands out with the inclusion of special abilities that add a unique touch. Players can choose from three different time modes: 1, 3, or 5 minutes, making the gameplay more dynamic. Additionally, the game has two special abilities—double speed and ball reversal—adding a strategic layer that differentiates it from other Pong games, increasing the challenge and excitement for players.
