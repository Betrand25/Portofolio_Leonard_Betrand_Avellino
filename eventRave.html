Eventrave

What’s this?

"ReventRave is a website designed for organizing a variety of events, including birthdays, weddings, and other special occasions. It provides users with a platform to customize and manage all aspects of their events, from venue selection to decor and budgeting. With personalized services, ReventRave aims to simplify the event planning process and ensure that every detail aligns with the client’s vision.

What was your role in the group?

This project was carried out in groups and the role I took on this project was as a Figma prototype design through Figma media.

What are the benefits of the project

its impact in helping communities in event management thus reducing the stress of creating and preparing events
