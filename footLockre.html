Foot Locker is an e-commerce site specializing in shoes from top brands like Nike, Adidas, and Puma. It offers a wide range of shoes for sports and casual fashion, allowing users to browse by brand, size, or style. The site also showcases bestsellers and exclusive collections, making it a go-to destination for shoe enthusiasts.

What was your role in the group?

This project was initiated as an individual assignment for the HCI course, completed independently as part of the college curriculum. It aimed to apply key concepts and principles learned throughout the course.

What are the benefits of the project

The impact of this project is that it makes it easier for people to search for and find shoe products according to their preferences. With a user-friendly interface, users can quickly discover shoes based on style, brand, or budget, enhancing their shopping experience.
